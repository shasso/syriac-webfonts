Demo of Marcus Syriac Fonts

These fonts were created by Esho Marcus and Sargon Hasso more than 20 years ago. They have evolved over time and were modified as Unicode Fonts in the late 1990's. These fonts can be used to create web pages that can be viewed on any device regardless whether your device has these fonts or not. This web technology will download these fonts onto your device and let you enjoy reading any content written in Syriac using these fonts.
